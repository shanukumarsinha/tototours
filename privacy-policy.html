Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Toto Tours respects your rights to the privacy of personal information and adheres to the following privacy policy. If you have questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy please contact tototours@gmail.com. Toto Tours reserves the right to amend this policy at any time.

General Browsing Information Personally Identifiable Information

We will explicitly notify you before we collect any personally identifiable information about you (e.g.. your name, email address, contact information, credit card number, etc.), and will not collect any information unless you choose to provide it to us. Toto Tours may share this information with its related entities, agents, and contributors, for the purpose of responding to any comments or questions you submit, and/or providing follow-up information about our company and its services.

Publicly Sharing Your Information

In certain instances, Toto Tours would like to publicly share the comments, and stories received from users of this site by posting this information on this site. We will not, however, publicly share any information we receive on this site without first receiving the express consent of the individual who submitted the information. Furthermore, we will not post information in any way that specifically identifies the sender of that information unless the sender approves it.

Toto Tours will never sell or provide to the general public or any other business enterprise any information collected from you during the normal course of business, including telephone numbers, email addresses and mailing addresses. Toto Tours endeavors to foster a close-knit camaraderie among tour group members, and provides contact information for each participant to other group members in advance of the tour as a means of enhancing that process. Information shared includes name, mailing address, home phone number and email address. Participants must notify Toto Tours at the time of tour registration if they want all or a portion of their contact information withheld from the other members of their tour group.
